FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is my baggage allowance?


Before you start packing your cabin and/or checked baggage, please make sure that you know what your size and weight limits are, in order to avoid any inconvenience or extra charges at the airport. The weight limits are:
  • Hand baggage: 12Kgs
  • Chek-in baggage: 20Kgs

Can you resend me my booking confirmation?


Missing your booking confirmation? Contact us, we will resend it to you.

What can I do if my baggage is delayed or lost?


The procedure followed in case of a delayed baggage depends on the days passed since you declared your missing bag at the destination's airport. We recommend to you to go as soon as possible at the office of the airport to signal a delayed or lost baggage.
